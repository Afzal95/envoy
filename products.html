How It Works

  1. Designate which desks are available

    Upload all of your desk data, then determine which desks are available to book, permanently assigned, or unavailable to maintain social distancing.

  2. Employees certify they’re healthy through Envoy Protect

    Employees must first complete your health questionnaire via Envoy Protect and meet your criteria to reserve a desk in the office.

  3. Employees book a desk from the Envoy app

    We’ll automatically assign a desk for the day, or employees can choose a different seat if they prefer. People can easily find where they are sitting that day in the Envoy app.

  4. Make smart decisions about your space

    You have a record of where employees are sitting so you know which desks to sanitize at the end of the day, and can optimize your space to be more efficient.

Adapt your space for safety and flexibility

You determine which desks are available to reserve based on your office’s capacity and social distancing guidelines. Easily add or remove desk availability at each office location as your local ordinances change.

Rely on easy and automatic desk booking

Employees book a desk for the day through the Envoy app after they complete your health questionnaire. We’ll automatically assign them a desk or they can choose a different seat if they prefer.

Use your space efficiently

Monitor how space is being used with Envoy analytics to make informed decisions to adapt your workplace to work better for your team—and save money on real estate. With Desks Analytics, you can make sure that workstations are used efficiently and no space is wasted.

Encourage cross-functional collaboration

Allow employees to change up where they sit each day to collaborate with different teammates or gain a fresh perspective. You can also designate specific neighborhoods so teammates can have the space to sit near each other.
